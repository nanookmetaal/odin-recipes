Ultimate Low-Carb Zucchini Lasagna

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1.5 large zucchinis, thinly sliced lengthwise
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 500gr ground beef
  • 1.5 cups low-carb marinara sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 220 grams ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 0.25 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • aluminium foil

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Lightly coat a baking dish with cooking spray. Set aside.

Step 2

Slice the zucchinis lengthwise into thin strips, about 1/4 inch thick. Lay them on a paper towel and sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of salt to help draw out moisture. Let sit for about 10-15 minutes, then pat them dry with a paper towel.

Step 3

Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.

Step 4

Stir in the marinara sauce, 1 teaspoon of salt, oregano, and black pepper into the cooked beef. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, then remove from heat.

Step 5

In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and ground nutmeg. Mix well until smooth.

Step 6

Assemble the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Layer some zucchini slices over the sauce, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture. Sprinkle a handful of mozzarella cheese over the ricotta. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of zucchini, meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.

Step 7

Cover the dish with aluminium foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

Step 8

Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
