Plov

This is a traditional Uzbek dish.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg lamb (cut into medium-sized pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati or long-grain rice
  • 3 large carrots (cut into thin sticks)
  • 2 large onions (finely sliced)
  • 4 cloves garlic (whole, unpeeled)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups water or broth
  • 1-2 dried chili peppers (optional)

Directions

Step 1

Rinse the rice in cold water several times until the water runs clear. Soak it in warm water for about 30 minutes, then drain.

Step 2

Heat the vegetable oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot or a deep pan over medium heat. Add the lamb pieces and brown them on all sides. This should take about 8-10 minutes.

Step 3

Add the sliced onions to the pot and cook until they are soft and golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.

Step 4

Add the carrots to the pot and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

Step 5

Season the mixture with cumin seeds, coriander seeds, turmeric, paprika, bay leaves, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine and cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.

Step 6

Add the whole garlic cloves and dried chili peppers to the pot. Pour in 4 cups of water or broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes, partially covered.

Step 7

Spread the soaked rice evenly over the top of the meat and vegetables. Do not stir. Add a little more water if necessary, just enough to cover the rice by about 1 cm. Increase the heat and bring to a boil again.

Step 8

Once the water is boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot tightly, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and all the water is absorbed.

Step 9

Once cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it rest, covered, for another 10 minutes.

Step 10

Fluff the rice gently with a fork and serve the lamb plov hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
